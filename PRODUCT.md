# Nuna Terra Perú — PRODUCT.md

## Product Purpose
Premium Peruvian travel agency offering curated tours, packages, and regional experiences across Peru's three geographic zones: Costa, Sierra, and Selva.

## Users
Discerning travelers (30–60) seeking authentic, well-organized Peruvian journeys. They value quality, culture, and meaningful experiences over mass tourism. They may be Spanish, English, Portuguese, or French speakers. They research carefully before booking.

## Brand
Warm editorial premium. The brand evokes deep earth, Andean textiles, colonial heritage, and wild nature — never resort-luxury clichés. Tone: poetic, confident, precise. Never generic travel-brochure copy.

## Register
brand

## Visual Identity
- Earth tones: `#5A3E2B` (tierra), `#2F5D50` (verde andino), `#D8B982` (arena), `#F7F1E7` (marfil), `#252525` (carbón)
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (700) — editorial gravitas
- Body: Montserrat — clean precision
- No rounded cards everywhere; layered editorial collages preferred
- No gradients on text; no glassmorphism; no hero-metric patterns

## Anti-references
Generic travel agency SaaS, stock-photo tourism sites, cookie-cutter card grids, neon adventure brands

## Content Structure
- Regions: Costa / Sierra / Selva (each has name, headline, description, hero/card images, SEO fields)
- Destinations: nested under regions, with altitude, bestTime, recommendedDays, idealFor
- Products: packages (multi-day) and tours (short); both have pricing, duration, destinations, itinerary
